Daniel Hergott

Daniel R. Hergott has been representing qui tam whistleblowers under the False Claims Act since 2011, when he graduated magna cum laude from Chicago-Kent College of Law. During law school, Mr. Hergott worked as an extern for The Honorable Ann Claire Williams of the U.S. Court of Appeals for the Seventh Circuit and as a clerk at the U.S. Attorney’s Office for the Northern District of Illinois and the Eastern District of Wisconsin. He has particular expertise in healthcare fraud and defense contractor fraud.

Admissions: Illinois; U.S. District Court (N.D. Illinois)
